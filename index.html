Partner With Us

Introduction

TAC Africa and WFP share a common vision for transforming Nigerian agriculture through technology-enabled solutions that empower youth and enhance food security across communities.

Background

Our collaboration stems from the urgent need to address food insecurity while creating economic opportunities for Nigeria's growing youth population through innovative agricultural practices.

With over 60% of Nigeria's population engaged in agriculture, yet facing low productivity, our joint initiative aims to revolutionize the sector with cutting-edge technology and capacity building.

Organizational Profile

TAC Africa is a leading agritech organization dedicated to sustainable agricultural development through technology integration, youth empowerment, and community engagement.

Our Global Services division has successfully implemented numerous agribusiness initiatives that have transformed farming practices and improved livelihoods across Nigeria.

Our Vision for Nigeria

Together with WFP and other partners, TAC Africa envisions a technology-enabled, youth-led agricultural ecosystem that ensures sustainable food systems, creates dignified livelihoods for smallholder farmers, and makes lasting contributions to Nigeria's economic development and food security.

Through this strategic partnership, we aim to scale our impact across all 36 states, transforming Nigerian agriculture into a modern, productive, and climate-resilient sector that feeds the nation and empowers its youth.

Our Five Pillars

TAC Africa's comprehensive approach to sustainable agricultural transformation is built on five foundational pillars.

Technology Integration

Implementing cutting-edge agritech solutions to modernize farming practices and increase productivity.

Youth Empowerment

Equipping young Nigerians with agricultural skills and entrepreneurial opportunities in agribusiness.

Food Security

Developing sustainable systems to ensure consistent access to nutritious food for all communities.

Economic Development

Creating value chains that generate income and stimulate local economies through agriculture.

Environmental Sustainability

Promoting climate-smart agriculture that preserves natural resources for future generations.

Innovative Technology Solutions

Our suite of technology-driven interventions is revolutionizing Nigerian agriculture.

Drone Technology

Multispectral sensors on drones provide precision crop mapping, health assessment, and yield prediction for optimal farm management.

IoT Sensors

Real-time monitoring of soil conditions, climate factors, and livestock health through connected sensor networks across farms.

Smart Irrigation

Automated watering systems that optimize water usage based on soil moisture data and weather predictions.

AI Analytics

Machine learning algorithms process farm data to provide actionable insights for precision farming decisions.

Weather Stations

Digital stations providing hyper-local weather data to inform planting, harvesting, and pest control schedules.

Traceability Systems

Blockchain-enabled tools that track produce from farm to market, ensuring quality and food safety standards.
